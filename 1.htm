Sony has announced that PlayStation 5 will launch in late 2020. Microsoft's Xbox Series X rival has not waited for its release to reveal its first secrets. A few months before its launch, here is everything we know about the next Sony home console.

The next Sony console will be released at the end of the year and will have the heavy task of succeeding the PlayStation 4. The current machine of the Japanese manufacturer crossed in summer 2019  the mark of 100 million copies sold worldwide, becoming the fourth home console to cross this symbolic milestone. The arrival of the PlayStation 5, and more generally of the next generation of consoles, is therefore highly anticipated by the players and the sector

In full mutation, the latter sees the cloud gaming emerge with a greater variety of players and an additional threat for Sony, Microsoft and consoles. For many, starting with Google, cloud gaming represents the future of video games, but “physical” consoles have not said their last word. It is in this context that the PlayStation 5, the ninth generation console from Sony, will be released
